Criteria for application to the Trust

Who Qualifies

Printers, former printers, print-related industries or their dependents who are in need and are within the charities objects/criteria. criteria

Object/Criteria

1. The relief of persons and their dependants who are in need, hardship or distress, or who are sick, convalescent, disabled or infirm and who are, or have been, employed in the graphical paper and media trades.
2. The trustees may relieve those eligible for assistance by: (a) making grants of money; or (b) providing or paying for goods, services or facilities; or (c) making grants of money to other persons or bodies who provide goods, services or facilities to those eligible for assistance.
The Trust does not give grants for building work or building repairs.

Area of Benefit

National (area prescribed by Governing Document).

Area of Operation

Charity operates inside and outside England and Wales.
Europe-United Kingdom

Registration

29 September 1966 Registered.

Classification

What: Medical/Health/Sickness, Disability, Relief of Poverty.
Who: Other charities/Voluntary bodies, Other defined groups.
How: Makes grants to individuals (includes loans). Makes grants to organisations (incl. schools, charities etc).
