History of the GPM Charitable Trust

The GPM Charitable Trust was formed on the 12th December 2001 bringing together the former Lloyd Memorial and NATSOPA Trusts. In August 2010 the Sheridan Trust, a Manchester-based printing charity, joined the GPM Charitable Trust.

The Trustees all have links to printing, packaging and papermaking and have a wide knowledge of the industry. They are dedicated to helping those that have worked in Graphical, Print, Media and Papermaking, and their families, who are in need of financial help.

Over the last twelve years the trust has made over 275 grants totalling over £158,000.
